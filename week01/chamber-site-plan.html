Site Name

Jefferson City Chamber of Commerce

This name represents a city where the chamber of commerce operates. "Jefferson City" was selected as the name because it is a well-known city in the region, and the word "Chamber" is included to convey the site's focus on local business promotion and community development.

Site Purpose

Promote Local Businesses: The site serves as a digital platform to showcase local businesses, provide resources for businesses to network and grow, and allow community members to discover and support these businesses.

Provide Community Resources: The site will offer key information on community events, news, and other resources that promote economic development, tourism, and local services that benefit both residents and visitors.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#003366): Used for the header, navigation, and footer background.
  • Light Green (#8CC63F): Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for body text.
  • Merriweather: Used for headings and subheadings.
  • Open Sans: Used for navigation and buttons.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Simple header with navigation, a hero section with a call-to-action, and sections for business spotlight and events.

Desktop View: Horizontal navigation, hero section, three-column layout for featured businesses, events, and member benefits, followed by footer.
